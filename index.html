About Me

Hi, my name is Goutham Swaminathan but most of my friends call me "Gooth". I am currently a junior studying in the AEDT academy at the Bergen County Academies, a magnet high-school located in Hackensack, New Jersey that was recognized as the #5 School in the Nation by Newsweek in 2015. I am involved in various clubs and organizations at BCA (Bergen County Academies) such as National Spanish Honor Society, hackBCA Organizing Team (Art sector), SPARK, and Diversity Alliance. In addition, I am also the co-president of the South Asian Club at my school, which hosts various events to spread South Asian culture while raising money to donate to India-based charities like Sahaita. As co-president, I am in charge of coordinating with our faculty advisor to organize events, introduce new members, and handle financials. I am also a senior counselor at E-Camp, a student-run engineering camp held for incoming freshmen in order to introduce them to basic engineering skills as well as help them have an enjoyable time. As a counselor, I, along with others, am in charge of organizing the camp, planning events, and creating projects for the students. Outside of school, I am a volunteer at Refresh Bolivia, currently acting as the cinematographer and am in the process of producing an RB-affiliated documentary about the system of public health in Bolivia. In addition, I also play an important role in maintaining the social media accounts of Refresh Bolivia and helping produce videos about Refresh Bolivia that are submitted to grant-based competitions. In my spare time, I maintain a small YouTube channel named "Gooth", which has garnered 250 subscribers and over 50,000 views. Thank you for visiting my website and I hope you enjoy it!
